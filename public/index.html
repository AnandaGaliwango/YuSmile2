About YuSmile Uganda

Who We Are

Created in 2018, YuSmile is a non-profit organization which promotes Education, Energy Access, Health and sustainable agriculture through Humanitarian aid and development projects in Uganda.

Our Vision

A Sustainable Impact for All

Our Mission

To create a positive impact in societies in need.

Objectives

  • To promote reading as a culture allowing children and caregivers an opportunity for long life learning.
  • To promote Energy Access in communities that are off grid and in unreliable grids.
  • To Promote a healthy living through best practices that will improve standards of human life.
  • To promote best practices in agriculture that will produce food while protecting the environment.

Focus Areas

1. Read2Child Program

We set out to donate children's story books through Health Centers that are located in disadvantaged communities, with a lot of uneducated families. This we believe will give a chance to their children get access to free children's story books that they otherwise would not have gained access to, that might help them improve their literacy levels but also the associated benefits of reading in their future.

2. Energy Access Program

More than 50% of the population in Uganda still used kerosene lamps and candles. We set out under this program to reach out to such communities and support them with cleaner energy solutions that are sustainable.

3. Empower Program

Skilling communities in various economic activities that will improve their livelihoods is important to us. Skills can be in best agricultural practices, Technical and vocational training support and healthy living practices needed in local communities.

Our Programs

YuSmile Uganda implements various programs aimed at creating sustainable impact in communities across Uganda. Our programs focus on education, healthcare, energy access, and community empowerment.

Read2Child Program

Our Read2Child program aims to promote reading as a culture by providing children's story books through Health Centers in disadvantaged communities. This initiative gives children access to books they otherwise wouldn't have, helping improve literacy levels and fostering a love for reading.

Energy Access Program

With more than 50% of Uganda's population still using kerosene lamps and candles, our Energy Access Program focuses on providing cleaner, sustainable energy solutions to off-grid communities and those with unreliable electricity.

Empower Program

Our Empower Program focuses on skilling communities in various economic activities to improve livelihoods. This includes training in best agricultural practices, technical and vocational skills, and healthy living practices needed in local communities.

Healthcare Access Program

Through mobile clinics and health education campaigns, we reach remote communities across Uganda with essential healthcare services and health education.

Women's Empowerment

We provide skills training and microfinance programs to help women achieve economic independence and become active contributors to their communities.
